It's easy to talk about things that don't matter. Do things that don't matter. To think about stuff that doesn't matter, hasn't mattered, and will never matter.
But you know what?
Things don't have to be like this.

What if I told you that you could operate by a code?
A code; that may not make you the world's most popular, or richest, or most famous man - but one that would make you uniquely your own.
That's the code I've outlined below for you.

Maybe you'll wonder about all of the constraints and disciplines. Is it all necessary?
No. No, it isn't. This is all completely voluntary. And d'you know something else?
You are going to fail at this, probably a lot. But that's okay.

The goal is not to reach some end point of perfection. You're human. That was never an option to begin with.
The goal, if we were to go by that vernacular, would be to stay on the path.
To be honest - the path might even end up getting you wordly success.
But that isn't the point. My final note - worldly success will come and go, but what the path will give you, is something greater, in my opinion. Peace.

    Internal Principia
  1. Be here now.
  2. Optimise systems, never make a goal, nor a plan . Life isn't a checkbox. It's an experience.
  3. Judge no-one. In their position, you'd do similar; maybe worse.
  4. Preach to no-one but yourself. Don't offer advice unless asked. You don't know much, yourself.
  5. Always be learning. You don't need an institution to learn.
  6. Be grateful for all the blessings. How can you want, when you've already got it all?
  7. Operate from a foundation of kindness.
    Disciplines
  1. Train in martial arts, and the additional components of resistance. Study martial arts for an hour a week at a specified time, with notes.
  2. Train in meditation as you would for your body. Mind matters. And be wary of excitement. Don't scorn it, but don't seek it either. Life is 90% boring, and that is beautiful - do nothing, whenever you're not doing hobbying or socialising.
  3. Have at least 2 periods per year of great immersion into writing. Document ideas into story.
  4. Learn about different technologies. Old, and new.
  5. Go to gatherings with people. Be a social creature, as best you can.
  6. Schedule - as time is important. Ensure daily doses of deep focus. Always do one thing at a time if it is possible.
  7. Increment your disciplines over time and improve.
    Negations
  1. Don't talk unnecessary shit.
  2. Don't do drugs, or drink more than 4X a year.
  3. Eliminate daydreaming or word-music or visualisation. With your condition they are triggers. This world needs you more than the one in your head.
  4. NoPMO. Be mindful of short-term foolishness and sex. It's often a logistics issue.
  5. Eliminate toxicity in all its forms. If a man is not good or switched on don't keep him. That doesn't mean materially. That means with respect to codes.
  6. NoSurf - social media, youtube; the internet is a hammer, a tool - not to be played with. Never read op-eds or bullshit articles. Discard fast media. Minimise sport that isn't combat-related - as study of the martial should be given.
  7. Negate the concept of self internally, unless socially and conversationally.
    Commitments
  1. Give back to causes throughout lifetime. Whether through donations, or your own foundations, try.
  2. Take what you need, and no more. Luxury is for the sake of others. Be frugal with yourself.
  3. Love your family and friends despite what they may think of you.
  4. Embody a man you'd be happy with your child knowing.
  5. See if you can be a force for inspiration through example. Small-scale, large-scale, whatever. Just live well.
  6. Never judge a man for hating you. Emotions are mirrors to souls.
  7. Try and ignore what the mainstream are doing. It's usually useless.

Additional Definitions

NoSurf - Never using the internet as a medium of entertainment. A tool only; scorning all social media platforms (including Reddit and YouTube).

Martial Arts - Boxing, MMA, Muay Thai, BJJ, Wrestling - basically anything where live sparring is an option.
