Deep, concentrated work is a prerequisite for an effective system. Without deep work - you have no system.
There will be deep work schedules for all modes of effort. Programming. Study. Business. Writing. Training. They will all have different seasons, but, throughout this - they will all be improved upon. They might just be the keys for your life. This is your true religion, sir. Not money, or fame, or anything else external. Strength, mastery, courage. These are internal characteristics that are true to the man.

    The emphasis is on practice and feedback, throughout deep work.

  1. Programming: Hours programmed - projects made. This is the key component, with personal projects. However, in the beginning, tutorials and docs can be aids.
  2. Study: Hours studied - grasp of material and application use. Fuck your grades. You study to apply, no matter what anyone else says.
  3. Business: Hours worked - value created. Even if you're not making money, if you're making progress towards either a product or even pivoting, you're okay.
  4. Writing: Hours written - words per hour/effective edits. The combination of focus, and writing/editing are key to progress.
  5. Training: Hours trained - effectiveness of movement and calmness of application. When you get better, you perform like a Samurai. None of this other, modern, Rocky shite. The aim is never a war. The aim is domination.
  6. Meditation: Hours practiced - presence throughout day. You'll fail at this a lot. But you also have the capacity to start again. A little better: day-by-day.
