To train with resistance is the complement to the martial arts. There are 6 moves you must perform, along with care taken to train for mobility, flexibility, endurance, with additional core and neck work.
These will provide the foundations of a beast. Not just any beast. A dad-beast. Because this is dad-strength we're talking about. No TikTok stuff.

  • Squat
  • Deadlift
  • Dip/Pushup
  • Press
  • Pullup/Chinup
  • Row
  • Core Training
  • Neck Training
  • Forearm Training
  • Weighted Circuit

These, good sir, will do it all for you. The programs you run will change, I imagine, but seek that these elements be incorporated.

Post Compounds

Core Work, Neck Work, Weighted Circuit

Definitions:

  • Neck Work - Flexion, rotation, and extension, building to a 100-rep-set.
  • Core Work - Flexion and rotation, building to a 100-rep-set.
  • Weighted Circuit - Circuit involving resistance (KBs etc.) to be performed at session's end for conditioning.
